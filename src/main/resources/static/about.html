About SmartJob

We're on a mission to redefine the recruitment process using intelligent matching algorithms that focus on skills, not just keywords.

Our Story

Built for the Modern Workforce

Founded in 2024, SmartJob was born out of a simple observation: the traditional job search is broken. Job seekers spend hours filtering through irrelevant postings, while employers struggle to find qualified candidates in a sea of applications.

Our platform uses advanced skill analysis and keyword-based matching to bridge this gap, ensuring that every connection is meaningful and productive.
